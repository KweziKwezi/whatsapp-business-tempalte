How to Order

To place an order, simply open our WhatsApp Business profile and tap on the "View Catalogue" button. Browse through our available products, choose what you’d like, and then tap "Message Business" to send us your order. Please include the item name, quantity, and any specific details we should know. We’ll reply as soon as possible to confirm your order and provide delivery or collection information. It’s quick, easy, and direct!
